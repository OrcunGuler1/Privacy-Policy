Privacy Policy — Circuit Connect

Effective Date: 2026-03-02

This Privacy Policy explains how Orçun Güler (“we,” “us,” “our”) handles information when you use Circuit Connect (the “App”).

1) Overview

Circuit Connect is designed to work without requiring you to create an account. We do not collect personal information to build profiles about you or to improve the App through analytics.

2) Information We Collect
A) Information You Provide

If you contact us by email for support, we will receive the information you choose to share (for example, your email address and the contents of your message). We use this only to respond to you.

B) Information Collected by Ads (If Enabled)

If the App shows advertisements, advertising partners may collect information automatically, such as:

Advertising identifier (e.g., Android Advertising ID)

Device information (device type, OS version, language)

Approximate location (derived from IP or device signals, depending on the ad provider and settings)

Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks, fraud prevention signals)

This data is collected by the ad provider to deliver and measure ads, prevent fraud, and comply with legal requirements. We do not receive information that directly identifies you, but ad providers may process identifiers under their own privacy policies.

C) Local App Data (Stored on Your Device)

The App stores gameplay data locally on your device, such as:

Run progress, stats, unlocks, meta upgrades, and settings

This data stays on your device unless you delete the App or clear its storage.

3) How We Use Information

Support emails: used only to reply to your request.

Ads (if enabled): ads are shown and measured by third-party ad partners; we do not use analytics to track you for product improvement.

Local gameplay data: used to run the game and remember your progress on your device.

4) Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in these cases:

With advertising partners if ads are enabled, as necessary to display ads

If required by law, legal process, or to protect rights and safety

5) Data Retention

Support emails: retained as long as needed to handle your request, then deleted when no longer necessary.

Local gameplay data: remains on your device until you uninstall the App or clear the App’s data.

6) Your Choices

You can remove local game data by uninstalling the App or clearing its storage.

You can control ad personalization (where available) using your device settings and Google/Apple ad settings.

7) Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required by your jurisdiction) without parental consent. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

8) Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information we handle. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

9) International Transfers

If you use the App outside Türkiye, information may be processed in other countries (for example, by ad providers). These transfers are governed by the third parties’ privacy practices.

10) Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the effective date above. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.
