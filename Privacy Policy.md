# Privacy Policy — Bureau of Lost Minutes

**Effective Date:** February 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how **Bureau of Lost Minutes** (“the App”, “we”, “us”) handles information.

---

## 1) Summary
- We do **not** collect or store personal information on our own servers.
- The App uses third-party services for monetization:
  - **Google AdMob** to display **non-personalized ads only** (**Banner** and **Rewarded** ads; **no interstitial ads**)
  - **RevenueCat** (via `react-native-purchases`) to manage in-app purchases/subscriptions (if enabled)
- We do not operate our own analytics, tracking, profiling, or advertising systems.

---

## 2) Data We Collect
**We do not directly collect** personal data or anonymous analytics through our own systems.  
This includes: name, email, phone number, precise location, contacts, photos, microphone/camera data, or custom analytics/telemetry.

---

## 3) Advertising (Non-Personalized Only) — Google AdMob
The App shows **non-personalized ads only**, meaning we do not request interest-based ads or ads based on your profile across apps.

Even for non-personalized ads, **Google AdMob** may process limited information to deliver ads, measure performance, and prevent fraud. This may include (depending on platform and settings):
- **Device and app information** (e.g., device model, OS version, app version)
- **IP address** (often used for security, fraud prevention, and approximate location)
- **Ad delivery and interaction data** (e.g., impressions, clicks)
- **Identifiers used for basic ad serving and integrity** (may vary by platform/device settings)

**Ad formats used:** Banner ads and Rewarded ads.  
**Ad formats not used:** Interstitial ads.

---

## 4) Purchases / Subscriptions — RevenueCat
If purchases/subscriptions are available in the App, we use **RevenueCat** to manage entitlements and purchase status.  
RevenueCat and/or the relevant app store (Apple App Store / Google Play) may process purchase-related information such as:
- product identifiers
- subscription/entitlement status
- transaction timestamps
- an app-instance identifier used to associate purchases with your installation

We do **not** receive or store your payment card details. Payments are processed by the app store and its payment providers.

---

## 5) Data Stored on Your Device
The App stores game progress **locally on your device** (for example, in on-device storage).  
This data stays on your device and is not uploaded to us.

If you delete the App or clear its storage, local progress may be removed.

---

## 6) Data Sharing
We do not sell user data.  
Data may be processed by **Google (AdMob)** and **RevenueCat** solely to provide ads (non-personalized) and purchase functionality.

---

## 7) Children’s Privacy
We do not knowingly collect personal information ourselves. The App uses third-party services described above; if you have concerns related to a child’s data, please contact us.

---

## 8) Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the **Effective Date** above when changes are made.
